For the final project of my CompTIA A+ certification course, I was given the opportunity to design, order, and build my own PC to be used for the remainder of my high school career. The entire shop divided into groups to compete for the best design. We had to create a professional presentation to persuade the class to vote for our design. This was designed to accurately simulated the process of bidding on a project for a client.

Specifications

RAM 8GB DDR3 667MHz
CPU 8 Core 3.1 GHz AMD FX-8120
Storage 2x 1TB SATA HDD
SATA DVD RW Optical drive

Excel Templates

Automated quote and purchase order excel templates I made to save time during the order process. The templates automatically create a matching Purchase Order, Quote, and Invoice based off of an Estimate.
